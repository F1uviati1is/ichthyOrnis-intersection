# ichthornis-intersection

A small experimental space where two researchers — an ichthyologist and an ornithologist — explore ideas at the intersection of their fields.

This repository serves as a sandbox for testing concepts, quick analyses, and data-driven insights about ecological patterns, rivers, birds, and the systems that connect them. Not everything here is polished or final — the goal is exploration rather than perfection.

If something here sparks an idea or you would like to collaborate, feel free to reach out.
